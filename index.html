About Us

The Alumni Association of K L University (KLUAA) has been functioning from 1985 at the University campus. The main aim of the Association is to maintain the link between University and Alumni and share their details of employment and achievements. The Alumni are helping the final year students in their project work and also about the employment opportunities in their respective organizations in India and abroad.
